Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqi

1963

Sympathizer Person of Pakistan

The well-wisher for whole Ummah, Mr. Qadri, SITARA-E-IMTIAZ is the Chairman of Saylani Welfare Trust, which is one of the biggest welfare organizations in Pakistan. But do you know the biography of the man who is behind this whole work and progress?

Bashir Ahmed Polani was born on March 3, 1963 in the month of Ramazan in the city of Shikarpur, Sindh. He belongs to a noble and religious family from the Bantawa Community and his father Abdul Ghaffar Polani was a successful businessmen.

He completed his schooling from Islamia High School Shikarpur and then travelled to Karachi in 1975 to pursue college & Islamic studies. In Karachi he got admission in Abdullah Haroon College from where he completed his intermediate education.

After completing college he pursued his Islamic studies from Darul-ul-Uloom Amjadia from where he successfully did his Dars-e-Nizami.

In early 1990’s Maulana Bashir Farooqi along with some other people started their charitable and social work. Later on May 15, 1999 he established a non-profit organization which was named ‘Saylani Welfare International Trust’.

From the establishment of Saylani Welfare till today he is serving as the Chairman, leading all the projects and work of the organization. What makes him more unique is the fact that he works voluntarily for the organization.

Along with his social work, Maulana Bashir Farooqui still continues to serve people through his scholarly knowledge. He is known for offering Istikhara solutions to people through TV program ‘Istikhara – Dukhi Dilo Ka Sahara’ on QTV.
